## ERP boundary

Receivables never imports peer ERP packages for mutations and never writes
sales order, delivery, payment, or journal tables. Customer/item identity uses
Master Data snapshots. Cross-module integration uses ports and versioned outbox
events. Receipt application references an existing posted Payment instruction;
Payments marks the instruction applied/rejected at the composition root.

## Invariants

- Every record belongs to exactly one organization.
- Draft invoices may be cancelled; posted invoices are corrected through credit notes.
- Invoice quantity cannot exceed remaining source quantity (sales order / delivery).
- Applications cannot exceed invoice open amount.
- Customer balance projections are rebuildable from posted source records.
- Public operations are authorized, idempotent, and concurrency-safe.
