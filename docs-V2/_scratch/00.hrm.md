# HRM Enterprise Completion Roadmap

## Final verdict

**Continue with the existing `@afenda/human-resources` architecture. Do not redesign or split the package.**

The package already has a substantial domain kernel:

* 286 commands
* 141 queries
* 106 HR mutation tables
* 99 permissions
* Memory and Drizzle store architecture
* strong time, leave, workforce-foundation and employee-relations foundations

The remaining work is a controlled completion program covering safety, parity, historical truth, product composition, integrations and operational readiness.

---

# Program objective

Complete an enterprise HRM that supports:

1. workforce identity and employment;
2. organization structure and assignments;
3. recruitment and hiring;
4. employee lifecycle;
5. leave and time;
6. compensation and benefits;
7. performance;
8. learning;
9. talent and succession;
10. compliance and employee relations;
11. workforce planning;
12. payroll handoff;
13. privacy, authorization and audit;
14. product Actions and operator workflows;
15. reporting, integration and production operations.
